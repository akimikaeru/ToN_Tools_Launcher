---AFK stealth jump---
This feature lets you "jump" in VRChat without leaving your current window.

F1: Turns the AFK script ON or OFF (beep sound for clarity).

Hotkey: Press your mouse button (XButton1) to jump.

Change Key: Click the [Hotkey] button in the app and press a new key to change it.
